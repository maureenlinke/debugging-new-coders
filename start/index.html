About the data

The Student Weight Status Category Reporting System (SWSCR) collects weight status category data (underweight, healthy weight, overweight or obese, based on BMI-for-age percentile). The dataset includes separate estimates of the percent of students overweight, obese and overweight or obese for all reportable grades within counties and/or regions in New York.

Three elements

Visualization types include:

  • Highcharts chart: top ten elementary schools with highest percentages of overweight and obese children.
  • Mapbox map: Mapping locations of schools with markers.
  • Datatables: Table of all schools in Erie County, New York.
